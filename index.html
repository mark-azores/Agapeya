ABOUT US

Who are we?

 Agapeya Towns by Elanvital is a 22-hectare project located in Brgy. Majada, Calamba, Laguna Its strategic location gives you access to places that matter. This vibrant community features twin home units, perfect for you and your family.


Who are the developer?

  Elanvital Enclaves provides management, sales and marketing services to its partner-developer Raemulan Lands, Inc., in various residential developments in Cavite and Laguna. To empower families to have the home they deserve, Elanvital Enclaves offers value-for-money quality modern homes suitable for growing middle-income Filipino families.


 From the French words Elanvital, which means soul or life force, and Enclave, which is about exclusivity. Elanvital Enclaves provides private, exclusive spaces and safe communities that raise the Filipino family’s quality of life. This philosophy is translated into the company’s logo, a parent and child shaped into a heart, fully knowing that it is the Filipinos’ love for family that serves as the topmost reason to dream bigger and invest in the future
